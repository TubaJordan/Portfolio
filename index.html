About Me

Jordan Nelson

Embarking on a dynamic shift in career trajectory, Jordan Nelson has delved into the realm of web technologies, now specializing in full stack web development. Although this venture marks a departure from his previous career path, Jordan's flexibility, adaptability, and passion for learning remain consistent.

Prior to this, Jordan's name resonated deeply within the marching and pageantry arts communities. Starting in 2008, he wore multiple hats ‑ from educator and clinician to designer and adjudicator. His involvement with notable organizations like the Bluecoats of Canton, Ohio, Phantom Regiment of Rockford, Illinois, the Arizona Academy of the Performing Arts of Tempe, Arizona, and being on faculty with the Arizona State University Athletic Bands speaks volumes of his dedication to the arts and his passions.

Music and education might have been his initial calling, but the challenges of full stack web development now captivate Jordan's interest. Yet, irrespective of the domain, his passion for excellence remains unwavering.

Jordan currently resides in Scottsdale, Arizona with his partner, Malia, and their two dogs, Rusty and Kolby.

Ghibli Movie Collection (React Version)

This application is a user friendly and visually pleasing app that allows a user to browse, sort, search, and favorite different movies created or produced by Studio Ghibli. Through the user experience, the user will notice a clean user interface that allows for responsive browsing across all platforms, including mobile, desktop, and for screen reading applications. Each user must create an account prior to using the app and will save their favorite movies, allow the user to update their profile information, and delete their account if desired. A easy to use navigation bar hold easy to use features such as sorting movies by various specifications, search for a specific movie, access their profile, return to the main screen, and logout.

Ghibli Movie Collection (Angular Version)

The Ghibli Movie Collection is a meticulously crafted application developed using Angular and enhanced with Angular Material, resulting in a vibrant and captivating interface. Users can effortlessly navigate, sort, and bookmark their favorite Studio Ghibli movies. Its design ensures a consistent and flawless user experience across various devices — from mobile to desktop and screen readers. Users are required to register, which opens up a suite of features: favoriting movies, updating profile details, and the option to delete their account when needed. The intuitive navigation bar offers quick access to functions like sorting movies by different criteria, managing profiles, returning to the homepage, and logging out.

Meet App

The Meet App is a serverless PWA crafted with React and rooted in TDD methods. Its key strength lies in integrating the Google Calendar API, allowing users to pull upcoming events effortlessly. Beyond listing, users can navigate and filter events across cities, gaining deeper insights. The app boasts a user-friendly design complemented by clear data visualizations. A hub for event exploration and planning, the Meet App ensures users stay updated and informed.

To Do List App

This JavaScript-based to-do list app focuses on practicality and user-friendliness. The clean interface allows users to easily add tasks, ensuring nothing important gets overlooked. If a task is completed, it can be removed with a simple click. For those who prefer to keep track of their progress, a double-click on a task activates a strike-through effect, marking it as done without removing it. This feature offers a quick visual overview of what's been accomplished. The app is designed to be both simple and functional, providing an efficient tool for anyone looking to keep their tasks organized without the fuss
